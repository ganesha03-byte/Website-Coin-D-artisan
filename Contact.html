Who We Are?

Coin d'Artizan is a leading e-commerce platform dedicated to offering unique and handmade artwork. Our mission is to create a platform where people can enjoy beautiful and handcrafted pieces at affordable prices.

We are passionate artisans dedicated to crafting exquisite pieces that celebrate individuality and sustainability. Our pieces are handmade with care, combining traditional techniques with modern design to create functional art for your home and lifestyle. We believe in supporting local communities and environmentally friendly practices, ensuring that each item not only enhances your space but also tells a story. Join us on our journey to transform everyday living with unique, handcrafted creations.

Coin D'Artizan has been created by a young students from University Of mauritius.
